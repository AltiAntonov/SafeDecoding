# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project
a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- being respectful and constructive
- accepting and giving feedback gracefully
- focusing on what is best for the project and community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, trolling, or insulting comments
- publishing others' private information without permission
- other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct
and may take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior
that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

To report an incident, please open a GitHub issue in this repository:
https://github.com/AltiAntonov/SafeDecoding/issues
